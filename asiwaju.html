Kaftan

Kaftan is traditional wear mostly worn in African countries like Nigeria. The outfit is simple and easy to wear. It can be worn to any event. The Kaftan is a traditional two-piece design with sleeves and a long pant.

Agbada

Agbada is a traditional robe of the Yoruba men of West Africa. It comes with an innercloth of varying lengths and it also comes with a pair of bottom wear in form of native trousers called Shokoto. It also is worn most times with different native Yoruba caps like Fila , Abeti Aja and so on.
